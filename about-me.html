DETAILS AND BIO

Mai Lee

Artist

BIO

Stunning. Seductive. Versatile. Mai Lee, pronounced (MY LEE), is every aspect of a star wrapped in one. Hailing from Saint Louis, MO, the R&B songstress began her musical journey at 9 years old when she enrolled into a performing arts elementary school. She fell in love with music listening to her favorite artists like, Mary J. Blige, Mariah Carey and SWV. Singing was the only time she felt free and at an early age, Mai Lee knew music was her passion. It wasn't long before Mai Lee caught the attention of super producer, Bradd Young (Lil' Wayne, Kelly Rowland, Bone Thugs N Harmony and Yo Gotti). Bradd Young and Mai Lee became an unstoppable musical force to be reckoned with. These two have molded a sound reminiscent of the 90's yet fresh and innovative with the instrumentals and vocal arrangements. Her original music has brought her opportunities to tour nationally and open up for some of the top entertainers in the industry. Mai Lee is ready to bless the world with intoxicating hooks, soulful vocals and alluring lyrics with her new single, "Nobody 1993" produced by Bradd Young & Vega. This melodic tune will undoubtedly sooth the ear of any listener! Find Mai Lee @maileemusic on IG, Twitter, Soundcloud, YouTube and Snapchat, @Mai Lee on Facebook.
